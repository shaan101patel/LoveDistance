# LoveDistance Product Brief

## Purpose
LoveDistance is a mobile-first couples connection app for long-distance relationships. It combines guided emotional intimacy, lightweight visual presence, and shared habit tracking into one product experience.

The app should feel private, warm, lightweight, and habit-forming without becoming noisy, clinical, or overly social.

## Product Vision
Create a daily relationship operating system for couples who want to feel emotionally close even when they are physically apart.

## Core Product Pillars

### 1. Emotional intimacy
The app should help couples connect through one thoughtful shared prompt per day, gratitude, reflection, and follow-up conversation.

### 2. Ambient presence
The app should support quick photo-based presence, low-friction reactions, and home-screen/widget-oriented visibility.

### 3. Shared growth
The app should help couples build healthy routines through shared and individual habits, streaks, goals, and calendar awareness.

## Primary User
- Long-distance couples.
- Mobile-first users.
- People who want something more intimate than social media and lighter than a journaling app.
- Users who value privacy, emotional safety, and simple daily rituals.

## Experience Principles
- Private by default.
- Fast to open and use.
- Feels warm, not clinical.
- Encourages consistency without shame.
- Prioritizes small daily actions over long sessions.
- Designed for iPhone and Android parity.

## MVP Scope
The first version should include:
- Couple pairing via invite link.
- Daily question prompt.
- Delayed reveal after both answer.
- Prompt thread with reactions and replies.
- Live photo sharing with lightweight caption/mood.
- Shared memory timeline.
- Shared habit calendar with mine / yours / ours habits.
- Streaks and shared goals.
- Notifications and reminder preferences.
- Deep links to specific content.
- Core privacy controls.

## V2 Scope
Build after MVP is stable:
- Voice notes in prompt threads.
- Weekly gratitude flow with photo.
- Sunday recap with top photos and LLM-ranked best prompt moment.
- Widget cards beyond latest photo.
- Emotional dashboard and trends.
- Calendar integrations.
- Offline drafting and later sync.
- Premium customization and exports.

## Non-Goals for Initial Build
- Public social feed.
- Multi-couple or group spaces.
- Complex video editing.
- Large creator-style content features.
- Desktop-first workflows.

## Success Criteria
A strong first release should make it easy for a couple to:
1. Pair in under 2 minutes.
2. Complete a daily prompt in under 1 minute.
3. Send a photo in under 10 seconds.
4. Track shared habits in under 30 seconds.
5. Revisit memories and milestones easily.

## Design Direction
- Mobile-first.
- Emotionally warm.
- Soft, elegant, minimal.
- Rose-gold accents with warm neutral backgrounds.

### Color palette
- Rose gold highlight: `#D89A84`
- Rose gold mid-tone: `#C77F68`
- Rose gold shadow/depth: `#AF6752`
- Light warm background: `#F6EFEA` to `#F3E9E3`

## Product Summary
LoveDistance should feel like a private shared home screen for a relationship: one part emotional check-in, one part instant presence, and one part shared progress tracker.
